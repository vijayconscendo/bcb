Why switch your business account to us?

Switch your Business bank account in three easy steps

  1. Complete a Call me back form.

  2. A consultant will call you and open your account for you. A card will be delivered to you or can be collected at a branch.

  3. After you have collected your card, you can register for Internet banking and transact immediately.

Due to rules and regulations we do not have authorisation to switch all debit orders, however we will assist with the debit orders we are able to, for the remaining debit orders you will be required to contact your current financial institution.

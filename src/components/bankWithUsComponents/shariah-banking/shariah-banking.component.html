OUR SHARI’AH ADVISORY COMMITTEE

To ensure that everything we do is in compliance with Shari’ah and internationally accepted best practices, our Independent Shari’ah Advisory Committee are scholars of international repute and include:

Mufti Zaid Haspatel

Mufti Zaid completed his Aalim Faadhil (with distinction) and his Iftaa at Darul-Uloom Zakariyya.

He currently lectures at Darul-Uloom Zakariyya and participates in the Iftaa department.

He currently serves on the Shari’ah Boards of Sentio Capital Management, 27Four Investment Managers and FNB Islamic Banking.

Mufti Yusuf Suliman

Mufti Yusuf Suliman serves as a member of numerous Shari’ah Advisory Committees, including but not limited to Standard Bank, Bank of Maldives, Discovery Health, Investec Bank, Capital Legacy & Alexander Forbes.

He developed the Islamic Banking training material for all South African Islamic Banks, having been contracted by BANKSETA.He is also a consultant to some Islamic Financial Institutions globally.

He is one of few Scholars in SA accredited by AAOIFI as Shari’ah Advisor and Auditor. He also holds Certificates in Qadha (adjudication) from Imarat Shari’ah, Patna Bihar (India) and has completed Iftaa (issuance of verdicts) at Darul Uloom Zakariyya, Lenasia (South Africa).

Qari Mohamad Sauleh Peck

Qari Mohamad Sauleh completed his degree in Higher Islamic Education, Aalim Faadhil (with distinction) as well as Ifta at the Darul Uloom Zakariyya.

He established the Fatimah institute in Houghton for adult women to specialise in the sciences of Arabic morphology, syntax, Quranic exegesis, Hadith and Jurisprudence.

He is currently the senior Imam at West street Masjid in Houghton, Johannesburg.
